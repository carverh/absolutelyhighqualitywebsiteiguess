Crystal Dream Planet by Saccstry

Description

There is a girl in the background with long green hair. The girl is in outer space. There is the moon, stars and saturn like planet in the sky. The girl is crying over a large planet, creating a river. The planet has red land with holes, crystals and eyes. The girl has a circle of floating crystals. The girl has crystals on her head. There are bones of dead armadillos on the planet and a space shuttle with two people standing next to it.

Analysis

The picture uses vibrant colors and has pinkish colors on the planet. The girl's colors are greenish and the sky colors are bluish. There is dimention to the picture.

Interpret

Παυλ and Πετροσ were on an expedition to a previously unknown planet. There had been reports of strange energy bursts coming from the planet. The trip to this unknown planet, named Σ-25, took around 10 months. When the explorers finally arived they saw a femoid with green hair crying. They asked her what was wrong. She responded:

όπως ήταν στην αρχή και πάντα θα ήταν ένας κόσμος χωρίς τέλος

The explorers were shocked by this statement, not knowing how to respond to this wise wisdom.
